Snorkeling with Turtles in Mirissa


Swim with Graceful Sea Turtles in Their Natural Habitat


Experience the magic of Mirissa’s ocean with our Snorkeling with Turtles tour. This peaceful yet thrilling encounter brings you face-to-face with gentle sea turtles gliding through crystal-clear tropical waters.

    What We Offer:

  • ✔ Free Pickup & Drop-off (within the Mirissa area)
  • ✔ Tour Duration: approx. 1.5 hours
  • ✔ Snorkeling Gear Provided – clean, safe, and easy to use
  • ✔ Professional Local Guides – one guide for every two guests (extra guides for larger groups)

At Mirissa Snorkeling Diving & Fishing Tours, your safety is our top priority. Our team ensures you're fully supported throughout the tour so you can enjoy the experience comfortably-even if it’s your first time snorkeling.
