Deep Sea Fishing Tours in Mirissa – Reel in the Adventure Beyond the Horizon


Set out on an unforgettable Deep Sea Fishing adventure designed for anglers of all skill levels. Whether you're an experienced fisherman or casting your first line, our expert guides will make sure you have a safe, fun, and successful day on the ocean.

  • What’s Included :

  • Free Pickup and Drop-off (in the Mirissa area)
  • Tour Duration: 3 Hours
  • All Fishing Gear & Equipment Provided
  • Snacks & Delicious Food Onboard – stay refreshed and energized
  • Life Jackets & Full Safety Equipment Included

Your safety is our top priority. All trips are guided by experienced crew members, and we provide all the necessary safety equipment for a smooth and secure experience.

Experience the thrill of deep sea fishing with the beautiful Indian Ocean as your backdrop. Perfect for solo adventurers, couples, or groups looking for a unique experience in Mirissa.

Book your Deep Sea Fishing Tour today with Diving Tours Mirissa – and take home not just fish, but unforgettable memories!

What You Can Catch :

Venture into deeper waters and reel in a variety of ocean species, including Tuna and other exciting game fish. Whether you're looking for a serious catch or a relaxing day at sea, this tour has something for everyone.
