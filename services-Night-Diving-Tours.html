Night Diving in Mirissa – Discover the Ocean’s Hidden Nightlife


Experience the thrill and mystery of the underwater world at night. Join our Night Diving Tour and explore the vibrant nocturnal marine life that comes alive beneath the surface after sunset.

  • What’s Included :

  • Free Pickup and Drop-off (in the Mirissa area)
  • All Diving Gear Provided – well-maintained for your safety
  • Guided Night Dive Starting at 7:00 PM

All tours include a full safety briefing and are led by certified dive professionals.

We prioritize your safety and comfort, ensuring that your night dive is both thrilling and secure. Get ready to witness glowing plankton, curious nocturnal fish, and a whole new side of the ocean you’ve never seen before.

Book your Night Diving adventure with Diving Tours Mirissa today – and dive into a magical underwater world after dark!

Licensed Divers :

Dive up to 15m–20m and enjoy a deeper, more immersive experience.

Special discounted rates for certified divers!

Non-Licensed Divers:

No license? No problem! You can still join and dive safely up to 3m with the close supervision of our experienced guides.

Important Info:

Minimum 2 persons required for booking.
