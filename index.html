About Us

At Mirissa Snorkelling Diving & Fishing Tours, we’re passionate about sharing the magic of the ocean. Based in the heart of Sri Lanka’s southern coast, we specialize in whale snorkeling, whale watching, and scuba diving adventures.

  • Expert Local Guides

    Our experienced team knows the Mirissa waters inside out, ensuring safe, exciting, and informative tours every time.

  • Top-Quality Equipment

    We provide clean, modern snorkeling and diving gear to ensure your comfort and safety.

  • Safe & eco-friendly adventures

    We prioritize safety and respect for wildlife while ensuring an unforgettable experience.

  • Free Underwater Photos

    Capture your magical moments beneath the sea — we provide complimentary underwater photography!

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our whale snorkeling, whale watching, and scuba diving tours in Mirissa.

How long does each tour last?

Our tours typically last between 2 to 3 hours, depending on the activity and sea conditions.

Is it safe for beginners?

Yes, all our tours are beginner-friendly. We provide safety gear and professional guides to assist you throughout the experience.

What marine life can I expect to see?

You may encounter whales, dolphins, sea turtles, tropical fish, and vibrant coral reefs during your tour.

Should I book in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking early to secure your preferred time, especially during peak season (November to April).

What should I bring for the tour?

We suggest bringing swimwear, a towel, sunscreen, and a waterproof camera. We provide all necessary gear and drinking water.
