About Us

Welcome to Mirissa Snorkeling Diving & Fishing Tours – your gateway to unforgettable marine adventures on Sri Lanka’s stunning south coast. We offer a range of ocean experiences including turtle snorkeling, whale watching, scuba diving, and deep-sea fishing, all led by passionate local experts.

  • Expert Local Guides

    Our friendly and skilled team knows every wave and reef around Mirissa. We ensure every tour is safe, professional, and full of local insight.

  • Premium Equipment Provided

    We use high-quality, well-maintained snorkeling, diving, and fishing gear to give you the best experience both above and below the water.

  • Safety & Sustainability First

    Your safety and the wellbeing of marine life are our top priorities. We follow eco-conscious practices to protect our ocean and its creatures.

  • GoPro Underwater Photos & Videos

    Leave the camera to us! We’ll capture your underwater moments with complimentary GoPro photos and videos - perfect souvenirs of your ocean journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our whale snorkeling, whale watching, and scuba diving tours in Mirissa.

How long does each tour last?

Our tours typically last between 2 to 3 hours, depending on the activity and sea conditions.

Is it safe for beginners?

Yes, all our tours are beginner-friendly. We provide safety gear and professional guides to assist you throughout the experience.

What marine life can I expect to see?

You may encounter whales, dolphins, sea turtles, tropical fish, and vibrant coral reefs during your tour.

Should I book in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking early to secure your preferred time, especially during peak season (November to April).

What should I bring for the tour?

We suggest bringing swimwear, a towel, sunscreen, and a waterproof camera. We provide all necessary gear and drinking water.
